HISTORY

Design Career Club is thrilled to host the 4th annual Design Career Fair at UC Davis Art Annex. Though the event is relatively new each has gone through considerable change which has allow us to experience one memorial fair after another. This is a look back at all the incredible people who made these experience possible.

It all began with our founder, Tiffany Trieu, an empowered individual who believed we can achieve community ambitions by cultivating our individual ambitions. She wanted to expand on the opportunities provided by general campus wide resources for design students. From this idea, the first Design Career fair came to fruition and was held in the SCC.

In 2016, Tiffany returned to lead the second annual career fair and the team saw a substantial success rate in the community. We realized we could run more than one event, we wanted to be a student organization.

The following year we decided to take on more events. This included Alumni Networing Day, Design Career Fair and Internship Night. We really wanted to expand our ability to bring together the design community. The student organization grew to be 30 members strong.

We hope to carry on the tradition and create more opportunity for our up-and-coming students to reach bold heights.
